About Us

Our Story

Founded over two decades ago, OSO Site Development and Septic began with a simple mission: to provide top-notch plumbing and landscape services that our customers could depend on. Through hard work, dedication, and a deep understanding of our clients' needs, we have built a reputation for excellence and reliability. Many of our clients have been with us for years, valuing the consistency and quality of our work.

Our Services

  • Plumbing: We offer a full range of plumbing services, from routine maintenance and emergency repairs to new installations. Our experienced team ensures each job is done right the first time.
  • Landscape Development: Transforming outdoor spaces is our passion. We provide comprehensive landscape design, installation, and maintenance services to create beautiful and functional environments.

Our Commitment

  • Experience and Expertise: With over 25 years in the industry, our team brings unmatched expertise to every project. We understand the unique challenges of the Southwest Florida region and tailor our services to meet those needs.
  • Community Focus: As a company deeply rooted in the Hispanic community, we pride ourselves on offering services that are both affordable and of the highest quality. Building strong, lasting relationships with our customers is at the heart of what we do.
  • Reliability: Our clients know they can count on us. Whether it's a small repair or a large development project, we are dedicated to delivering dependable service every time.
